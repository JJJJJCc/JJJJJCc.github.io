Sakura: VR Game

This is an exploration game in VR formate, where users can talk around and find objects around. Trigger them to wake uo this silent world. Also, you are able to input MIDI signal to play music in the scene. Audio effect like chorus, delay, echo and reverb can be added to the room where you play the music.

With You

This is an ineractive audio-visual unity project, which simulates the trip to Death Valley. The intro scene leads you crossing the sea and flying to the sky and then touch the stars, which makes you enter the main theme. The scene building is in low-poly style.

Music Flow

Instrument-karaoke application in a Galaxy-look scene. You can load any your favorite music and then you can improvise the music by using the keyboard typing. At the mean time, you can enjoy the audio-visualization on the screen.



Ocatve

Designed a "penrose step" shape of music sequence and rendered graphics with Unity engine. Real-time audio sequencer functionality and the interactivity that users are able to input the notes by using keyboard.
