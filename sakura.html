Sakura

This is a musical exploration VR piece, which allows you to trigger your VR world with the music played by your MIDI keyboard. Initial inspiration arose from my desire that playing instrument in a beautiful place (not just in practice room). Meanwhile, I love the peaceful and Zen vibe of Japanese environment, so this is why I choose Japanese garden as my VR aesthetics.

The simple background music is at back, but you have to play your own music to wake the whole silent world, which intends to be the mirror of your own experience and emotion. If you are happy, you will play happy song, or if you are sad, maybe you will play something sentimental. People said they feel healed and peaceful after playing this, so it is also a good choice for calming down and meditation.

This project used VRTK, Unity and MIDI keyboard.


#HTC VIVE VR headset #Unity #AKM320 keyboard
