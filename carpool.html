CarPool

This is an interactive road trip kareoke experience. I still can remember the day that I had a road trip with my best friend towards Los Angeles from Palo Alto, when we chated, laughed and sang songs with CD. I want to keep this memory so this is why I made this interactive road trip kareoke experience.

The mountains dances with the car music while our voice can trigger different light in the car. You would have fun when singing songs and seeing beautiful scenary besides you! Although due to Unity built-in microphone lantency, the audio playback is bad, so using another audio engine could be a solution.
