Workshop Intro

In this workshop, participants first acquired the introductory knowledge and skills to implement an audiovisual step sequencer that synchronizes rhythmic events and animated visual expressions and then learn how to extend it further as an interactive music application. The workshop also presented both historically important artworks and recent interesting examples in audiovisual expressions, aiming to help students broaden and deepen their understanding of the concept and the history of audiovisual expressions of the kind. By the end of this workshop, students created their own rhythms with our own interactive audiovisual step sequencer.
